My suggestions are the following

1) Apply all the patches listed at

http://www.nwchem-sw.org/index.php/Download#Patches_for_the_27746_revision_of_NWChem_6.6

2) Recompile from scratch (that is after typing "make clean") without redefining FOPTIMIZE and CC. In other words, just type

make FC=ifort
